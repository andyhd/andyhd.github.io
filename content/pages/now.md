## Location
I moved to rural South Oxfordshire five years ago and I can no longer imagine
going back to London.

## What I am doing now
I spent the last year pretending my work on an AI team was "AI-adjacent" while I
buried my head in the sand. A brief stint working with an "all-in" client shook
me out of my complacency. I am now in full-throttle catch-up mode.

- Railing against the introduction of the software equivalent of the Jacquard
  loom and struggling to accept the end of coding as a craft. It is a painful
  wrench to admit that a core part of my identity is becoming a relic.

- Setting up my Home AI Lab and testing local models. I am currently running
  Qwen 3.5 27B on my 32GB MBP M2 Max. It is surprisingly good.  I am saving up
  for a beefy GPU and as much VRAM as I can afford.

- Building a coding agent from scratch. This is much more about learning how
  agents work than the end result, which is lucky given how it currently
  performs.

- Neglecting my Common Lisp and Raylib implementation of Thrust.

## Learning
I moved rapidly from believing this AI stuff is bullshit to realizing I need to
get up to speed. I am now worrying that I am skirting dangerously close to being
an AI evangelist. There is none so zealous as a convert.

- Japanese. I am currently just using Duolingo and need something more complete.
- Karate. I just started and I am grading for 9th Kyu very soon.

## Reading and Listening

Christmas. I am currently reading Harry Potter with my daughter. We are enjoying
the story while trying to reconcile that with how hateful the author is.

I listen to my old favorites like Boards of Canada when I get a choice. Most of
