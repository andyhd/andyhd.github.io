## Location
I am still enjoying the idyllic rural South Oxfordshire countryside. It has been five years since we moved here and I still cannot imagine going back to London.

## What I am doing now
My day job involves LLMs and agents yet I still managed to realize in January that I am hopelessly behind. I have spent the last few months trying to catch up.

### Home Lab
I have been setting up local models to see what they can do. I am currently enjoying Qwen 2.5 72B.

### Agents
I am building a coding agent from scratch. This is mostly a learning exercise because I want to understand the plumbing. It is much more about the process than the end result which is lucky given how it currently performs.

### The Great Transition
I am slowly letting go of crafting code and learning how to direct agents instead. It is a massive wrench to stop typing the characters myself but I am told this is the future. 

My Common Lisp and Raylib project called Thrust has been gathering dust since January while I fell down this particular rabbit hole.

## Learning
I have moved rapidly from believing this AI stuff is bullshit to realizing I need to get up to speed. I am now worrying that I am skirting dangerously close to being an AI evangelist. There is none so zealous as a convert. Feeling like a beginner again is a total rollercoaster.
